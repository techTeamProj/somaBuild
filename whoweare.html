Our Mission

Soma Coffeehouse and Juice Bar's mission is to provide our customers, staff, suppliers and community with an unparalleled and complete coffee, tea and juice experience in an environment steeped in understanding, knowledge, skill, service and mutual respect.

At Soma, we believe in connecting people who love good quality coffee. We are committed to promoting and contributing to our local community, building strong relationships with our suppliers, showing true respect to our environment, and providing a fun and fulfilling work environment for our employees.

Our Friends and Partners

Soma prides itself on continued activism within the Bloomington community. Each month we spotlight a community organization and sponsor that organization in some way. Stop by Soma to see our Community Spotlight Board, and check our website monthly to see our current Spotlight Organization.
