Get Here

Visit all or one of our dynamite locations!

Soma Kirkwood is on the corner of Kirkwood and Grant. The entrance is on the east side of Kirkwood Manor below the Laughing Planet Cafe. This building is also home to Cactus Flower, Moonstones, and TD's. You can enter from inside of the LP too by taking the stairs down one level. We've a few parking spots along the south side of the building and a bike rack out front.


Soma East is located on the corner of 3rd and Jordan in Jordan Square sharing a parking lot with Dragon Express and Hair Jordan. We also have a bike rack on the west side of the building.
